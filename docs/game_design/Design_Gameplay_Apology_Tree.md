# Design: Arborescence des Excuses (Delay Apology)

Ce document contient l'arborescence validée et les scripts de dialogue à générer sur ElevenLabs pour les différentes annonces de retard "Delay Apology".

**Dossier d'exportation cible :**
`assets/sounds/EN_FD_Rowan/TO_PA/Delay/`

---

## 1. 🌩️ WEATHER (Météo)
**Dossier :** `assets/sounds/EN_FD_Rowan/TO_PA/Delay/Weather/`

*   **Fichier :** `EN_Rowan_Delay_Weather_01` *(Orages/Brouillard à destination)*
    *   **Texte :** "Ladies and gentlemen, the captain speaking. We are currently being delayed due to poor weather conditions at our destination. We'll update you as soon as the conditions improve and we're cleared to depart."
*   **Fichier :** `EN_Rowan_Delay_Weather_02` *(Météo au départ)*
    *   **Texte :** "Ladies and gentlemen, this is the flight deck. Our departure is unfortunately delayed due to the heavy weather directly over the airport. Air traffic control has put a temporary ground stop in effect for our safety."
*   **Fichier :** `EN_Rowan_Delay_Weather_03` *(Dégivrage / Forts vents)*
    *   **Texte :** "Ladies and gentlemen, from the flight deck. We are experiencing a slight delay while we wait for the de-icing equipment to finish preparing our aircraft for a safe departure, exacerbated by the strong winds today. Thanks for your patience."

    *   **Texte :** "Ladies and gentlemen, this is the captain. We have been issued a slot restriction by Air Traffic Control, which means we have a specific departure time we must wait for. We'll be on our way as soon as our window opens."
