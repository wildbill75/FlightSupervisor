# Design Gameplay: Fuel Economy & Efficiency (Future)

## Overview
Rewards pilots for efficient fuel management and accurate planning based on SimBrief estimates.

## ⛽ Metrics
- **Burn Accuracy**: Comparison between `Planned Trip Fuel` and `Actual Trip Fuel`.
- **APU Management**: Penalties for excessive APU usage at the gate after ground power is available.
- **Engine Start Timing**: Encouraging standard single-engine taxi or delayed starts during long queues.

## 🏆 Scoring Impact
- **Maintenance/Operations**: Fuel efficiency bonuses are added to the "Operations" pillar.
- **"The Green Captain" Badge**: Unlocked for completing a flight where actual burn is within 1% of planned burn.
