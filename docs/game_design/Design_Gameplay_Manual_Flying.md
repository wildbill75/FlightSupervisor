# Design Gameplay: Manual Flying & Precision (Future)

## Overview
Encourages "Airmanship" by rewarding the pilot for hand-flying the aircraft during critical phases.

## 🕹️ Metrics
- **AP OFF Timer**: Tracks the cumulative time the Autopilot is disconnected below 10,000 ft.
- **Approach Precision**: Bonus for flying the final approach (4,000 ft to Touchdown) manually without exceeding glide/localizer limits.
- **Bank/Pitch Smoothness**: Higher rewards for manual flying if G-Force deviations remain minimal (< 0.1G delta).

## 🏆 Scoring Impact
- **Safety**: Rewards "Airmanship" bonuses.
- **"Hand of God" Badge**: Unlocked for significant manual flying time in a single sector.
